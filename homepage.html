An open World Exploration Game

Tainted Star is a world exploration game, where you walk, explore the forest, venture across valleys and climb the highest mountains for an unforgettable experience. An amazing sound design and the stunning visuals guarantee this game to be next on your wish list.

Play Now

Features of the Game

Explore a huge world

Immersive Open World

Explore the magnificent outdoors by boat, off-road vehicle, or on foot. Discover turquoise springs, majestic mountain peaks, hidden ponds, and charming outposts in your search for the perfect fishing spot.

A unique experience

Diverse Biomes

Take on a variety of unique biome types in a unique and immersive experience. Each biome has its own appearance and characteristics that influence its environment.

Become a Master Explorer

Character Progression

Create your own character and complete various missions. Buy clothes and craft your own explorations.

Play alone or with friends

Multiplayer Mode

Join an unprecedented exploration experience and play online in co-op mode for up to 10 players. Meet new Survivors and explore the open world together or alone.
