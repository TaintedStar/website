An open World Exploration Game

Tainted Star is an atmospheric open world exploration game that invites you to lose yourself in a vast, mysterious landscape. Traverse ancient forests shrouded in mist, cross windswept valleys echoing with forgotten whispers, and scale towering mountain peaks that pierce the sky. Along your journey, you'll uncover hidden secrets and collect lost souls—remnants of a forgotten past, each one adding depth to the world's haunting story.

With breathtaking visuals and a rich, immersive soundscape and effect that evolves as you explore, Tainted Star is more than a game—it's an emotional journey. Whether you wander beneath the starlit canopy or brave the icy silence of the mountains, every step offers a moment of wonder and reflection.

If you're seeking a meditative yet mysterious adventure wrapped in beauty and atmosphere, Tainted Star is the next experience you won't want to miss.
